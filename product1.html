The Odyssey

Photobook

Personal

In 2019 I did this little photobook inspired by "The Odyssey" a film by Florence + The Machine from “How Big, How Blue, How Beautiful”, that show all the chapters about the film. Divided into 9 chapters, the photobook shows the lyrics and visuals of the songs featured in the film. These are previe of a few spreads of the project.
